Services

Service Information

SKILL9 is dedicated to preparing students with the essential software skills and knowledge necessary to connect with local and global communities, fostering cultural competence and adaptability. Our objective motivates us to create budget-friendly and dependable software training courses that encourage participation from a diverse range of learners.

Student Profile Analysis

Our academy conducts student profile analysis to better understand each student's strengths, weaknesses, and learning style. This helps us tailor our educational approach and support services to maximize each student's potential.

Career Counseling

At our academy, we offer career counseling services to help students explore career options and develop a career plan. Our experienced counselors work one-on-one with students to identify their strengths and interests, and provide guidance on career pathways.

Internship Program

Our academy offers an internship program that provides students with valuable hands-on experience in their field. This program allows students to apply classroom knowledge in a real-world environment, while also making valuable industry connections.

Job Placement Assistance

Our academy provides job placement assistance to help our graduates launch successful careers. Our dedicated team works with employers to identify job opportunities, and helps students prepare for interviews and land their dream jobs.

Placements

Our academy has a strong track record of successful placements for our graduates. Through our network of industry connections and career support services, we help our students launch rewarding careers in their chosen fields.

Guidance

At our academy, we provide guidance and support to help students achieve their academic and career goals. Our experienced faculty and staff are committed to helping students navigate the educational journey and achieve success.
